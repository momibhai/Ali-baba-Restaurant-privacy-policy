Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ali Baba Restaurant ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you interact with our WhatsApp chatbot or services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: We receive your WhatsApp phone number to identify and communicate with you.
  • Messages: Messages you send via WhatsApp are stored temporarily to provide support and respond to your queries.
  • Order & Contact Data: If you place an order, we may store your name, address, and order details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your messages and support requests.
  • Process and fulfill food orders.
  • Improve our chatbot and customer experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Chatwoot (our support platform) for message handling.
  • Internal staff for order processing.

4. Data Retention

We retain your message history for 30 days, unless required by law or for order fulfillment.
